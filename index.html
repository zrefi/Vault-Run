How to Play

MOVEMENT: W, A, S, D keys. Click screen to look.
DEFEND: Press SPACEBAR to blast an EMP shockwave that freezes the guard for 3 seconds! Must be unlocked in the Shop first — it won't work otherwise.

MUTE: Press M or click the speaker icon (top-right) to mute/unmute sound.

GOAL: Find all 3 Keys using your compass helper arrow, collect coins for bonus points, and escape before time runs out!
BEWARE: The guard gets faster and angrier with every key you collect!

TIP: Once a run starts, check the menu bar along the top of the screen — it tracks your timer, keys, coins, lives, and ability status at a glance.
